Payment

In the LEGRAND network, payments are made only in tenge.
We accept payments from individuals

In retail stores:

  • cash;
  • VISA, MasterCard and American Express bank cards;
  • gift cards;
  • gift certificates;
  • bonus cards.

In the online store:

  • cash at the office of the online store or upon delivery of goods;
  • bank cards VISA and MasterCard, only online when ordering goods with delivery;
Cashless payment for legal entities
An invoice for non-cash payment can be obtained at any retail store at the checkout or generate automatically by placing an order on the site www.legrand_v_astane.kz.
  • Pay only after confirming the availability of goods.
  • Delivery is carried out after receipt of money on the account of the store.
  • To receive the goods from the payer, the original accounting power of attorney is required (Form D1) and the identity card of the recipient.
